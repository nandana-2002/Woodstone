WHO WE ARE

Woodstone designers is a complete one-stop solution for building your dream residence. Established in the year 2011, we have in-house teams that undertake all aspects of construction like architecture, construction, interior design, and landscaping. Apart from these, we also have full-fledged villa projects happening all over Kerala. aim as the best construction company in Trivandrum is to build homes and Villas that are affordable for everyone. We strive to provide all the amenities that a luxury villa or home provides in various budgets that will suit everyone.
